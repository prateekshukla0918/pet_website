Our Boarding Policies

Vaccination Requirements

All pets must be up-to-date on vaccinations. Proof of vaccination is required at check-in to ensure the health and safety of all our guests.

Check-In/Check-Out Times

Check-in is between 8am-11am and check-out is between 3pm-6pm. Early drop-off or late pick-up can be arranged for an additional fee.

Food & Medications

Please bring your pet's food and any medications with clear instructions. We follow their regular routine to minimize stress.

Reservation & Cancellation

A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking. Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in may incur a cancellation fee.

Personal Items

We encourage bringing a favorite toy or blanket to help your pet feel more comfortable during their stay with us.

Behavior Assessment

First-time boarders may undergo a brief behavior assessment to ensure we can provide the best care and appropriate playgroup placement.
